### Registration → Paid Conversion

| App Tier | Male CVR (Free→Paid) | Female CVR (Free→Paid) | Avg. Time to Convert |
|----------|---------------------|----------------------|---------------------|
| Casual (Tapple-tier) | 22–28% | 8–11% | 4.2 days |
| Mid-tier (Pairs-tier) | 31–38% | 12–16% | 6.8 days |
| Premium (Yoitoki-tier) | 18–24% | 6–9% | 11.3 days |

> **Why premium tier has lower CVR but higher LTV**: Premium users spend more time evaluating. Once converted, their ARPU is 2.8–4.1x higher and churn rate is 40% lower.

## Match Quality Metrics

| Metric | Industry Avg. | Premium Segment |
|--------|--------------|-----------------|
| Match rate (weekly) | 3.4 per user | 1.8 per user |
| Message response rate | 31% | 54% |
| First date conversion | 8.2% of matches | 19.7% of matches |

> **Key insight**: Premium platforms have *lower* match volume but *dramatically higher* quality ratios. Users are less overwhelmed, more intentional.

## Churn Analysis

### Top Churn Reasons (Exit Survey Data)

| Reason | % of Churned Users |
|--------|-------------------|
| Found a partner (positive churn) | 31% |
| Poor match quality | 28% |
| Too expensive / no value felt | 19% |
| Fake profiles suspected | 14% |
| App UX/bugs | 5% |
| Other | 3% |

### Churn Risk Signals (Early Warning Indicators)

- No match within first 7 days → 3.2x churn risk
- Zero messages sent in week 2 → 2.8x churn risk
- Profile completeness <60% → 4.1x churn risk
- No login for 5+ consecutive days → 2.4x churn risk
