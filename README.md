## 💡 Industry Trend Analysis

### Trend 1: Premiumization
The ¥5,000+/month tier is growing 3x faster than the free/freemium tier. Users increasingly pay for curated, high-intent environments. Platforms like [yoitoki.jp](https://yoitoki.jp) that focus exclusively on serious relationships are capturing this demand.

### Trend 2: AI-Powered Matching
- 67% of users under 30 prefer AI-driven match recommendations over manual browsing
- Swipe fatigue is a documented phenomenon — average session length dropped 22% since 2021
- Next-gen platforms are integrating personality analysis, not just photos

### Trend 3: Safety & Verification
Post-2022 regulatory pressure has accelerated mandatory ID verification. Platforms with stricter verification see **+34% retention** vs. unverified platforms.

### Trend 4: Serious Intent Shift
Post-pandemic behavioral shift: users self-reporting "marriage-focused" intentions increased from 38% (2020) to 61% (2024). This benefits platforms positioning around 真剣交際 (serious relationships).
