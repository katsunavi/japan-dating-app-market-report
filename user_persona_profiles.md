# 👤 User Persona Profiles — Japan Dating App Market
### 日本マッチングアプリユーザー ペルソナ研究

> Research by [yoitoki.jp](https://yoitoki.jp) | Based on survey data n=2,847

---

## Overview

Five core personas account for **91%** of active dating app users in Japan. Understanding these archetypes is essential for product positioning, content strategy, and UX design.

---

## Persona 1: 「真剣派サラリーマン」 Ken (32, Tokyo)

**Archetype**: Serious marriage-seeker, male, urban professional

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Age | 30–35 |
| Income | ¥5.5M–¥8M/year |
| Location | Tokyo, Osaka, Nagoya |
| Apps used | Pairs, Omiai, **Yoitoki** |
| Monthly budget | ¥5,000–¥10,000 |

**Motivations**:
- Wants to get married within 2–3 years
- Tired of casual apps with low-intent matches
- Values efficiency — limited time due to work
- Willing to pay premium for quality

**Pain points**:
- Fake profiles / time-wasters
- Women not responding seriously
- Overwhelmed by choice, poor match quality
- Unclear about which app is "serious enough"

**Content that converts him**:
- "マッチングアプリで本気で結婚できた体験談"
- Comparison articles focusing on 真剣度・結婚率
- Efficiency-focused guides ("忙しい社会人のための婚活術")

---

## Persona 2: 「キャリア婚活女子」 Yuki (29, Osaka)

**Archetype**: Career-focused woman, selective, marriage-minded

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Age | 27–33 |
| Income | ¥4M–¥6M/year |
| Location | Major cities |
| Apps used | with, Pairs, **Yoitoki** |
| Monthly budget | Free–¥2,000 (on female-free apps) |

**Motivations**:
- Wants equal partner, not just provider
- Values intellectual compatibility and values alignment
- Marriage is a goal but not at expense of career
- Wants to feel safe and respected on the platform

**Pain points**:
- Inappropriate messages / harassment
- Men who don't take the relationship seriously
- Feeling like "product" on swipe-based apps
- Pressure from family about age

**Content that converts her**:
- "アラサー女性が婚活アプリで成功した方法"
- Safety and verification features explained
- "対等なパートナーシップを築くための婚活"

---

## Persona 3: 「再婚活中年」 Hiroshi (42, Fukuoka)

**Archetype**: Divorced, re-entering dating market, cautious

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Age | 38–48 |
| Income | ¥6M–¥12M/year |
| Location | Regional cities + Tokyo |
| Apps used | Zexy, 結婚相談所, **Yoitoki** |
| Monthly budget | ¥8,000–¥15,000 |

**Motivations**:
- Had negative past relationship experience
- Financially stable, ready to commit again
- Wants someone mature and serious
- Values privacy and discretion

**Pain points**:
- Stigma around divorce in matchmaking
- Worried about being scammed
- Doesn't understand modern app culture
- Wants human support, not just algorithms

**Content that converts him**:
- "40代 離婚経験者の婚活完全ガイド"
- Privacy and security features
- Success stories from similar demographics

---

## Persona 4: 「ゆるっと恋活女子」 Mika (24, Tokyo)

**Archetype**: Young woman, open to relationship but not marriage-focused yet

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Age | 22–27 |
| Income | ¥2.5M–¥4M/year |
| Location | Tokyo, Yokohama |
| Apps used | Tapple, with, Pairs |
| Monthly budget | ¥0–¥1,500 |

**Motivations**:
- Wants to meet interesting people
- Open to serious relationship if right person
- Enjoys the social/discovery aspect
- Not ready to commit to "婚活" framing

**Pain points**:
- Creepy or pushy men
- Being immediately asked for LINE ID
- Feeling pressured toward marriage timeline

**Note for yoitoki**: This persona is **not the primary target** for premium positioning, but may graduate to Persona 2 within 2–4 years. Top-of-funnel content can capture her early.

## Persona 5: 「ハイスペック狙い女性」 Rie (34, Tokyo)

**Archetype**: Success-oriented woman seeking high-earning partner

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Age | 30–38 |
| Income | ¥3.5M–¥5M/year |
| Location | Tokyo, Kanagawa |
| Apps used | **Yoitoki**, 結婚相談所, Paters |
| Monthly budget | ¥3,000–¥8,000 |

**Motivations**:
- Clear criteria: income, education, appearance
- Believes in complementary partnership model
- Has tried multiple apps, frustrated by results
- Wants a platform that vets male members seriously

**Pain points**:
- Fake income claims / no verification
- Low-effort men on "premium" apps
- Expensive apps with no quality guarantee

**Content that converts her**:
- "年収確認済みの男性と出会えるアプリ比較"
- yoitoki's verification process explained in detail
- 高収入男性の婚活成功事例
