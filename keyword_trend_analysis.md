## Executive Summary

The Japanese dating/matchmaking search landscape is undergoing structural shifts:
- **Volume is growing** in high-intent, serious-relationship queries
- **Casual dating keywords** are plateauing or declining
- **Premium/高級 segment keywords** show the highest YoY growth (+30-44%)
- Mobile-first queries now represent **78%** of total search volume in this category
